Dry Cupping & Fascia Blading

Increases circulation, releases myofascial restrictions, and encourages tissue relaxation

Dry cupping involves placing cups on intact skin to create suction, which increases circulation, releases myofascial restrictions, and encourages tissue relaxation.

Fascia plays a key role in a horse’s proprioception. Because fascia is richly supplied with nerve endings, it sends constant messages to the brain about tension, movement, and position. This allows the horse to sense where its body and limbs are in space, adjust posture, coordinate movement, and respond accurately to the rider’s cues. Essentially, fascia acts like a communication network that helps the horse stay balanced, coordinated, and aware of its body during every step.

Cancellation Policy


As of February 1st 2026 there is a couple of policy changes.


Due to travel and cancellations leaving open spots on the day. We will be following deposit and cancellation policies more strictly for bookings.

At time of booking please send your best email, phone and address so we can send you the invoice for confirmation deposit.

Deposits for Bookings

There will be a 50% deposit, this will be required to secure the appointment- this is required in 24 hours of booking the appointment. 🔐

This helps avoid last-minute cancellations and keeps my travel routes efficient so every horse gets their allocated time. This deposit is deducted from the final appointment price.

If deposit isn’t paid, your spot is still open and can be secured by someone else.

Cancellations

  • ‼️48 hours + notice = cancellation is free, and any deposit is refunded.
  • ❗️24 hours of booking= deposit is non-refundable and the full session fee applies as the cancellation is considered late and an invoice will be sent for full payment.
  • ⁉️No-shows = full session fee applies and an invoice will be sent. Deposit is non-refundable

With this new policy in place, it will allow us to reduce no shows and last minute cancellations that impact our small business.

We are here to help your horses, please help us by giving us notice and book in🗓️
